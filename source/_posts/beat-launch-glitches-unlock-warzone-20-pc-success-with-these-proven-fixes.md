### Fix 4: Change NVIDIA settings

 NVIDIA control panel lets you fine-tune your in-game graphics. When there are visual issues, you can change the NVIDIA control panel settings to fix the problem.

1. Right-click the Windows desktop and select**NVIDIA Control Panel** on the pop-up menu.
2. Click**Manage 3D Settings** in the left panel.
3. Find**Antialiasing – Mode** on the right panel, change the setting to**Off** .
4. Click Adjust desktop color settings on the left panel. Make sure all settings are set to default.
5. Restart your PC and relaunch the game.

 If it’s not working, you’d better follow[fix 5](https://tools.techidaily.com/drivereasy/download/) to install the old NVIDIA driver.
