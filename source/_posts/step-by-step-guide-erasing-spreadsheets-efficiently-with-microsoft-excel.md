##  Remove an Excel Worksheet Using the Right-Click Menu

 To quickly delete a worksheet from your workbook, use an option in Excel's [right-click](https://desktop-recording.techidaily.com/updated-the-art-of-recording-fun-6-techniques-to-document-minecraft-for-2024/) menu.

 Start by launching your spreadsheet with Microsoft Excel. Then, at the bottom of your workbook, find the sheet to delete.

![Locate the sheet to delete.](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/06/1-select-unwanted-sheet.png) 

 Right-click the worksheet to delete, and from the menu that opens, choose "Delete."

![Click "Delete" in the menu.](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/06/2-delete-unwanted-sheet.png) 

 If your selected sheet has no data, Excel will delete it immediately.

 However, if your sheet contains any data, you will see a prompt. To continue with deletion, then in this prompt, click "Delete."

![Choose "Delete" in the prompt.](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/06/3-sheet-delete-prompt.png) 

 And that's it. Your chosen worksheet is now gone from your Excel workbook.
