| **Operating System** : | Windows 7 64-bit or newer                                             |
| ---------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Processor** :        | Intel Core 2 Quad Q9400 @ 2.66 GHz \| AMD Athlon II X4 650 @ 3.20 GHz |
| **RAM** :              | 4 GB                                                                  |
| **Graphics Card**      | ATI Radeon HD 5850 or NVIDIA GeForce GTX470 with 1GB VRAM             |
| **Hard Drive** :       | 2 GB                                                                  |
