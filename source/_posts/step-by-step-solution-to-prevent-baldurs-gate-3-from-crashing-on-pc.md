## **Method 6: Initialize the PS4 to fix the PS4 freezing**

 In addition, initializing your PS4 can also reset the system and solve the problem.

**Note** :  
 1\. When you initialize the system software, all settings and information saved on your PS4 system are deleted. This cannot be undone, so make sure you do not delete any important data by mistake. Deleted data cannot be restored. It’s recommended to**back up your data into PS plus or USB drive** before the initialization.  
 2\. **Do not turn off your PS system** during initialization. Doing this might damage your system.

 1) Go to PS4 **Settings** \> **Initialization** \> **Initialize** **PS4** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/img_5a571f9eebce2.jpg)

 2) Select **Full** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/img_5a571fc9dcd29.jpg)

3) Follow the instructions to complete.

4) After finishing, restore all your backup data, and reinstall the games and applications.

5) Try the games to see if it works now.
