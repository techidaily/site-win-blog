**1)** Run **Outlook** .

**2)**  Click the **File**  tab.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/2019-10-31_15-11-41.jpg)

**4)**  Click the **Data Files tab** . Then, select **your account** and click **Open File Location** .
