### Fix 2: Run the game as an administrator

 This quick fix is more like a general solution for all the games that cannot be launched properly, and it‚Äôs very easy to apply. As long as you have the administrative privileges to run the game, you should give this fix a try.

 1) Locate the **executable file** of Star Wars Jedi in your PC. Right-click on it and select **Properties** .

### Fix 5: Stop overclocking

 To anyone in pursuit of ultimate gaming experience, it‚Äôs quite common to speed up the CPU/GPU a notch or two.
