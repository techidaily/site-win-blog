## Fix 1: Change language on Steam

 This fix should resolve the issue with the missing voiceovers. Follow the guide and you’ll get back the audio.

1. Launch Steam.
2. Go to the Steam**Library.**
3. Right-click Dying Light and select**Properties** .
4. Choose Language, then change the language to English and close the tab.
5. Repeat the process, but this time pick your language.

 If you’re already in English, try another language to see if the voices are in this language, then turn back to English.

## Fix 4: Change your sound settings

 If you don’t have in-game audio from your headset, changing sound settings may work for you.

1. Right-click your**Sound** icon on the Taskbar.
2. In the**Playback** tab, double-click your default device.
3. Click the**Advanced** tab.
4. Change the**Default Format** to**DVD Quality** .
5. Click**OK** to save changes then reboot your system.
6. Launch the game to check the problem is solved or not.
