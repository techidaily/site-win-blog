### Fix 1: Meet_FM19_ ‘s minimum specs

| **OS:**        | Windows 7 64-bit or newer                                                                |
| -------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Processor:** | Intel Pentium 4, Intel Core or AMD Athlon – 2.2 GHz +                                    |
